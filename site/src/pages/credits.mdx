## Icons

Anta's icon set is built on the shoulders of several excellent open-source icon libraries. We're grateful to the maintainers and contributors of these projects:

- <a href="https://lucide.dev/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Lucide</a> — the modern, beautifully consistent icon family that supplies the majority of Anta's icons (ISC license).
- <a href="https://feathericons.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Feather</a> — the predecessor that Lucide forked from; a portion of icons trace their lineage back to Cole Bemis's original strokes (MIT license).
- <a href="https://blueprintjs.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Blueprint</a> by Palantir — additional product-UI glyphs covering domains the previous two don't (Apache License 2.0).

Full license texts and per-icon attribution are reproduced verbatim in the package's <a href="https://github.com/antithesishq/anta/blob/main/NOTICES.md" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><tt>NOTICES.md</tt></a>.
